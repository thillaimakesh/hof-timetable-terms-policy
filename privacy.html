Privacy Policy

This Alexa skill "Hof Time Table" does not collect or store any personal information from users. All timetable data is retrieved from publicly accessible calendars provided by Hochschule Hof.

We do not track, log, or share any user queries. The skill only processes the department name, date, and time for the purpose of fetching class schedules.

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer at: tsudakaran@hof-university.de
